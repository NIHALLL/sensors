How AquaRoot Works

A closed-loop sensing and actuation pipeline that monitors, decides, and acts — completely autonomously.

STEP 01
💧
Tank Level Sensing
Float or ultrasonic sensors continuously report MIN and MAX water levels, triggering the pump and preventing dry-run damage or costly overflow.
STEP 02
🌱
Soil Moisture Monitoring
Capacitive soil moisture sensors scan root zones in real time. When moisture drops below the configured threshold, irrigation begins automatically.
STEP 03
⚡
Automated Actuation
Relay-controlled pump and solenoid valves open or close based on sensor logic. No manual switching — the system acts before damage occurs.
STEP 04
✅
Feedback & Shutoff
Once soil moisture reaches the target level, the valve closes and pump stops. Precise cutoff eliminates waterlogging and energy waste.
